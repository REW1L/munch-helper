# Battle View

A battle view:

1. Set the battle name
2. Add a monster with its combat strength
3. Add characters to the battle
4. Add/remove a modifier for one side (decreasing and increasing strength)
5. Add/remove a modifier for another side (decreasing and increasing strength)
6. Change a character
7. View the result of the battle (in progress, finished)
8. Conclude the battle
9. Discard the battle
10. Go back (doesn’t affect the battle state)
